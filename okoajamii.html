About Us

Okoajamii is a community-based financial structure created to empower villagers and town residents through savings, contributions, and accessible loans. We believe in collective growth and financial independence.

Our Services

  • Savings: Safe and secure individual and group savings plans.
  • Loans: Affordable community loans for emergencies or development.
  • Contributions: Regular contributions for group investments and events.
